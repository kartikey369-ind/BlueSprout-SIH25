Rooftop Rainwater Harvesting Tips

Check Your Roof

Basic roof assessment for rainwater collection

Check Your Roof

  • Ensure your rooftop is suitable for rainwater collection
  • Keep it clean and free of debris to maximize water quality
  • Check for cracks, leaks, or structural issues
  • Consider roof material - metal, tile, or concrete work best
  • Ensure proper slope for water runoff

Maintain Gutters & Pipes

Keep your collection system clean

Maintain Gutters & Pipes

  • Regularly clean and inspect gutters, downspouts, and storage tanks
  • Prevent blockages and contamination
  • Install gutter guards to reduce debris
  • Check for rust or corrosion in metal components
  • Ensure proper drainage and flow

Collect Rainwater Efficiently

Optimize your collection system

Collect Rainwater Efficiently

  • Install tanks or barrels to capture rainwater from your rooftop
  • Use first-flush diverters to remove dust and dirt from initial rainfall
  • Calculate optimal tank size based on roof area and rainfall
  • Install overflow systems for excess water
  • Consider multiple collection points for large roofs

Use Water Wisely

Maximize your water savings

Use Water Wisely

  • Use collected rainwater for gardening, flushing toilets, cleaning
  • Install separate plumbing for non-drinking purposes
  • Consider filtration for potable use if needed
  • Monitor usage patterns to optimize collection
  • Implement water-saving fixtures and appliances

Recharge Groundwater

Help replenish underground water

Recharge Groundwater

  • Direct some rainwater to recharge pits, trenches, or soakaways
  • Help replenish groundwater levels
  • Install percolation systems in suitable soil areas
  • Consider borewell recharge for deeper aquifers
  • Consult local groundwater experts for best practices

Monitor & Spread Awareness

Track progress and inspire others

Monitor & Spread Awareness

  • Check water storage levels regularly
  • Clean tanks periodically and repair leaks immediately
  • Track water savings and environmental impact
  • Encourage your community to adopt rainwater harvesting
  • Share success stories and best practices
